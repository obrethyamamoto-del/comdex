By tokenizing real-world assets like Gold, Silver, Platinum, and Oil, we enable decentralized trading that is fully asset-backed, transparent, and Sharia-compliant. Every transaction is tied to tangible commodities, ensuring real value — not speculation.

### Platform Pillars
- **Precious Metals**: Gain exposure to Gold (Au), Silver (Ag), Platinum (Pt), and Palladium (Pd) with institutional-grade liquidity.
- **Energy Commodities**: Trade tokenized Brent Crude Oil (ABRN) with real-time pricing from global markets via Pyth Network.
- **Institutional Vaulting**: Every unit is backed 1:1 by physical inventory stored in secure vaults in Dubai (DMCC) and London.
- **Altai USD (AUSD)**: Gold-backed stablecoin serving as the primary settlement currency for all trades on the platform.
- **Participation Pools**: Stake your assets and earn real-yield rewards from platform trading fees and revenue sharing.

## Participation Pools: Monetizing Assets & Revenue Sharing
Altai Participation Pools offer a sophisticated mechanism where users turn their physical metal holdings into active yield-bearing instruments. By depositing metal-backed assets into these pools, users provide the essential collateral that powers the ecosystem and participate in the protocol’s global success.

### 1. Metal-to-Liquidity Mechanism
- **Asset Vaulting**: Participants deposit metal tokens (AXAU, AXAG, etc.) directly into Participation Pools, unlocking the latent value of physical metals without requiring a sale.
- **Collateral Rewards**: In exchange for providing high-grade collateral, users are rewarded with AUSD, which can be used for further trading or as a liquid cash-out option.

### 2. Institutional Revenue Sharing
- **Transaction Fee Distribution**: A significant percentage of every trade executed on the Altai Exchange is redirected directly to Participation Pool contributors.
- **Premium Vaulting Yield**: Revenue generated from storage fees and minting/redemption premiums is shared proportionally among metal depositors.
- **Real Yield Performance**: Participants receive shares in AUSD, creating a continuous stream of yield derived from actual market activity, not inflationary printing.

### 3. Capital Efficiency (Double-Benefit)
- **Physical Asset Exposure**: Users maintain full exposure to the price appreciation of the physical metal (e.g., gold price increases) while it is vaulted.
- **Active Revenue Stream**: Earn a share of the protocol revenue Sharing in AUSD, maximizing the capital efficiency of your idle physical assets.

### Revenue Sources Powering the Participation Program
- **Trading & Swap Fees**: A small percentage of every transaction on the Altai Commodities Exchange is allocated to the participation pool.
- **Treasury & Ecosystem Revenue**: A portion of ecosystem-wide income, including platform fees, contributes to yield distribution.
- **Arbitrage & Market Operations**: Real-time hedging and price-balancing mechanisms generate consistent profits by capturing market inefficiencies.
- **Institutional Services**: Enterprise clients and liquidity partners accessing Altai’s RWA infrastructure generate additional B2B income streams.

## High-Performance Triple-Oracle Architecture
Price accuracy and manipulation resistance are the core pillars of Altai’s institutional-grade trading ecosystem. Altai utilizes a robust Triple-Oracle Architecture that cross-references global data with proprietary safety protocols.

### The Triple-Feed Infrastructure
- **1. Pyth Network**: Institutional-grade, low-latency price feeds directly from top-tier financial institutions, ensuring precious metal and energy markets align with real-time global spot prices.
- **2. RedStone Oracle**: High-frequency update model optimized for commodities, providing a secondary layer of price validation that remains resilient even during extreme market volatility.
- **3. Altai Proprietary Oracle**: An internal "Anti-Manipulation" filter that cross-references external feeds against internal liquidity parameters to block unfair execution attempts.

### Anti-Manipulation Guard
If a discrepancy is detected between the three data sources, advanced safety buffers protect traders from unfair execution. This triple-check system significantly reduces the risk of price manipulation during low liquidity or high volatility periods, ensuring that Altai remains the most secure platform for hard asset trading.
